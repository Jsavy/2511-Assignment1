Our packages are specially designed to help your children succeed!


Here at Ustin Brothers, we strive to get children excited about web development through the use of exciting tools and exciting projects. Our professional instructors have years of experience in web development and have a passion for teaching younger generations. They are here to teach your children the necessary skills to develop them as web developers further. Our packages are specially designed to teach your children the fundamentals of web development in a fun and exciting way.


The Free Package

Kick start your children's love for web development with our free package. The free package teaches your child the fundamentals of HTML and how to create their first website. Our professional videos will give them the necessary skills to document their projects so other developers can read their websites. This is done to help reinforce good development practices at a young age.

The Basic Package

The basic package gives you all the same amazing features and lessons as the free package and includes the ability to style your website with CSS. Teaching your children CSS will allow them to further style their websites than they can with regular HTML. This package will advance your child's development skills bring them to the next level. With the ability to work at your own pace, it creates a less stressful environment for your children to learn in.

The Premium Package

The premium package is the ultimate package to get your kids introduced to web development. Everything from lower packages is included as well as with this package you get more one-on-one time with our professional developers. Our teachers will provide your child with not only the fundamentals of web development but will also offer handy tips and tricks to help them create websites more efficiently and effectively. These extra tips and tricks coupled with the fundamentals give your child the best start to their web development dreams.
